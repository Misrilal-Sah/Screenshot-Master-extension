🔒 Data Collection

✅ We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal user data.

These extensions operate entirely on your local device. No information is ever sent to any external server, third party, or cloud service.

💾 Local Storage Only

The only data stored by these extensions is saved locally on your device using Chrome's built-in Storage API:

Theme preference Color history Panel state Screenshot data (temporary)

This data never leaves your browser and is used solely to improve your experience within the extension.

🔑 Permissions Explained

activeTab — To capture or inspect the page you are currently viewing.

scripting — To inject inspection or capture tools into the active page.

storage — To save your preferences locally on your device.

clipboardWrite — To let you copy colors and CSS values with one click.

📡 Third Parties & Analytics

We do not use any analytics, advertising, tracking pixels, or third-party services of any kind. There are no SDKs embedded from external providers.
